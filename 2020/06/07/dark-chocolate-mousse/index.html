Dark Chocolate Mousse

Jun 07, 2020 in cooking, desserts

Don’t you just love chocolate? I know I do!
Here is a recipe for a dark chocolate mousse, quick and easy to make, with a special ingredient. Check it out!

Ingredients

Mousse:
  • 100 gr dark chocolate (of your preference)
  • 100 gr butter
  • 200 gr heavy cream
  • 200 gr Greek yogurt (10% fat)
  • half a teaspoon of chilly powder (crazy right?)
Toppings:
  • raspberries
  • berries mix
  • strawberries

Steps

1. Add heavy cream to a mixing bowl and use a mixer to obtain whipped cream. Store it in the fridge until you reach Step 3.

2. Add the chocolate, butter and chilly powder into a bowl. Place the bowl over a saucepan of simmering water (the bottom of the bowl should not touch the water) and stir in occasionally. Keep the bowl over the saucepan until the butter and chocolate are melted and the mixture is homogenous, then set the bowl aside.

3. Add the Greek yoghurt and the whipped cream into a large bowl and mix them together.

4. Pour the melted chocolate and butter over the cream and mix them together with a spoon. Mix slowly with large movements such that you obtain an aerated cream.

5. Place the obtained cream in serving bowls or glasses and store in the fridge for at least one hour before serving.

6. Serve the mousse with fruit toppings of your choice.

Checkout the gallery below for guiding photos.

You might find it strange to use chilly powder in desserts. But actually it is used in a lot of chocolate-based recipes because chilly accentuates the flavour of the chocolate.

Have fun!
