Salmon on Sea-Salt Bed

Nov 19, 2020 in cooking, main course

Yes yes yes. Fish is healthy. Even though I don’t like it as much as… chocolate for example, I try to eat it from time to time.
I find salmon really tasty and easy to cook, so here is my favorite recipe.

Ingredients

Salmon:
  • ~800 gr salmon fillet
  • 500 gr sea salt
  • pepper to taste
Side-dish:
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 bunch of asparagus
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • salt to taste

Steps

1. Spread the salt evenly in an oven tray.

2. Cut the salmon fillet in servable portions and then place the pieces in the tray with skin side down. Season the salmon with pepper. Bake it in the oven at around 220 degrees Celsius until it looks done. I usually test it with a fork. If it flakes easily under the pressure of the fork then it is done.

3. Wash and cut the asparagus.

4. Spread the olive oil evenly in a pan (I used a grill pan) and add the asparagus. Sprinkle the asparagus with salt and cook it 3-5 minutes on each side.

5. Serve immediately with the salmon. I added 2 fried eggs as a bonus. Because I deserve it.

Yes… I know. You feel like something is missing… The SAUCE, of course! That is why I added the fried eggs with a runny yolk…
I was too eager to eat the salmon and I skipped the sauce. However, if you have the patience to prepare a sauce, I recommend a hollandaise sauce with this dish.
