Kitchen "redo"

May 06, 2020 in DIY

Hello there my loyal readers (me, myself and I).

This evening I want to share with you one of my DIY projects: painting the kitchen tiles. Yup, you read that right.
There was no way I could change the tiles in my kitchen (too messy, to pricey and the cabinets were already set in place).
So I bought some (special tile) paint and I started playing with the paint-brush.
