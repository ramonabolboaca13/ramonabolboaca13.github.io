Healthy Cheesecake

May 17, 2020

Are you in the mood for something tasty, yet something healthy?
Here is a recipe for a tasty cheesecake, quick and easy to make.
Check it out!

Ingredients

Crust:
  • 150 gr cashew (optional: roasted and salted)
  • 150 gr dates
  • 100 gr melted butter
  • optional: salt to taste
Cream:
  • 300 gr cream cheese
  • 300 gr Greek yogurt (10% fat)
  • the juice of 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
Toppings:
  • berries mix
  • strawberries and bananas
  • raspberries
  • optional: 1 tablespoon of chia seeds

Steps

Crust:

1. Add dates, cashews and salt (if cashews are not already salted) to a food processor and mix until you obtain the desired consistence.

2. Add the crust into the desired container.

3. Spread the melted butter over the crust.

4. Store the container in the freezer until you prepare the cream.

Cream:

5. Add all ingredients into a big bowl and use a spoon to incorporate until you obtain a homogenous cream.

6. Poor the cream over the crust and use spoon to spread it evenly.

Toppings:

7. Place the desired fruits over cream.

8. Optional: If you are using frozen fruits you may also sprinkle a tablespoon of chia seeds over to them (the chia seeds will absorb the extra water).

Store in the fridge and serve whenever you feel like it!
